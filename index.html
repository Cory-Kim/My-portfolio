Who I am

Junior Software Developer

Since I was young, my dream has been to become a doctor. I don’t know if it’s because of the unknown influence from my parent as my parents wanted me to become a doctor like many parents in Asia do. As I go to school, I noticed that, unlike most other students, I weirdly liked Math more than all the subjects. I felt like all other subjects require me to memorize new words and concepts while in Math, I use few formulas to draw out the answer. I especially liked how I can solve complex problems in step by step. As I age, I begin to accept the fact that I wasn’t smart enough to become a doctor and I studied nursing at university. At this point, I still felt like I should at least get a job in the medical field. But, as time goes, I became more and more depressed as I just didn’t find my studying fun. One day, I was watching Youtube, and learning about basic programming came up as my recommended video. Without much thinking, I clicked the video and watched the whole 5 hours video. After watching the video, I was surprised that I learned a lot by just watching the Youtube video. I felt like it’s interesting how you build a program step by step just like Math. From this day, my programming journey started. It was a bit late but I am glad that I finally found something that I like. I decided to pursue the dream of becoming a programmer. So far I have learned Java, C, C++, PHP, Node JS, Jquery, Javascript, CSS, HTML, SQL, Bootstrap, Git/GitHub, and Linux. I’ve started learning new languages like MongoDB, and Angular. I am still very enthusiastically grabbing onto any other programming languages and frameworks.
